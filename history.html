History


Guitars have been around for more than 400 years, with the oldest guitar being made in 1590, originally called the vihuela. There are many variations of the guitar, all of them with a unique period of invention in history.

Variation of Guitar Time of Ivention
Vihuela 1590
Bass Guitar 1930
Electric Guitar 1936
